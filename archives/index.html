Brief

  • Associated Professsor in the TianQin Research Center for Gravitational Physics at Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU) from Feburary 2021. My research focus on the interplay fields between particle cosmoloy and particle astrophysics.

Research

  1. Gravitational wave cosmology/astrophysics

    • phase transition gravitational waves from dark matter, baryogenesis and other new physics mode.
    • gravitational wave compact binary and its implication for dark matter and modified gravity.
    • primoridal gravitational wave from inflation.

  2. baryogenesis

    • Electroweak baryogenesis, asymmetric dark matter, baryogenesis with dynamics CP violation, new baryogenesis scenario

  3. New approach to explore dark matter by SKA and FAST

    • Radio signals of ultra-light dark matter(axion, dark photon…) at SKA and FAST

  4. precise calculations

    • collider physics,loop calculations,effective field theory, Higgs physics.
